 Responsibility
Potential System Instability Warnings

As a user of Windows Update Restored, it is important that you understand the disclaimers provided by us. Our disclaimers serve as a precautionary measure to inform our users of the potential risks of using the Windows Update Restored websites. While we, the Windows Update Restored team, strive to provide an excellent updating experience for you, there is always a possibility of encountering issues such as damage, bugs, or data corruption on your computer(s) while using our platform.

Liability Exemption

Neither the Windows Update Restored team nor Microsoft Corporation can be held responsible for any damages that may occur as a result of using the Windows Update Restored websites. This disclaimer is put in place to protect both the Windows Update Restored team and the users from any unforeseen consequences that may happen during the usage of the restored websites. Despite our efforts to ensure the reliability and safety of the restored websites, there is always a possibility for complications to occur.

Backup Precautions

To avoid the risks associated with using the Windows Update Restored websites, we highly recommend that you backup important data before making use of the restored websites available here. This precautionary measure can help safeguard your valuable information in the event that any issues arise during the updating process. By taking the time to backup your data, you can minimize the potential impact of any damages that may occur while using the service.

Bug & Issue Reporting

In the event that you do encounter data corruption or damages while using any of the Windows Update Restored websites, it is important that you report these issues to the Windows Update Restored team. You can do so by letting us know in either our Discord Server or our MSFN topic. By providing feedback on any problems that you experienced during the service, the team can work towards fixing any bugs or issues that may have caused the data corruption or damages. Your input is valuable in helping the team improve the service and ensure a better user experience for all users.

We always make sure to not use any of the aforementioned elements in our software or content.
